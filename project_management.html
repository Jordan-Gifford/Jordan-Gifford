Project Management

I first studied about Project Management during my Level 4 qualification. I learnt about the Project Management methodology and theoretically put the method into practise during my level 5 qualifications by forming a team. I found the methodology really fun to follow, as each section was a long drawn out process with a lot of barriers to get through to actually arrive at a deliverable.
During my study of Project management, in order to understand the theory, I had to produce a piece of software which was never going to be physically produced. I learnt about each step of the project Life-cycle in conjunction with the Software Development Cycle. I also learnt about the importance of Burn Down charts,Gantt charts and carrying out a Critical Path Analysis.
